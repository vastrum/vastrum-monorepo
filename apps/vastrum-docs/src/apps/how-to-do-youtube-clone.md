# How To Do Youtube Clone

A streaming video platform like Youtube could be implemented on Vastrum.

Videos could be stored as "1 second" batches on key value, could do streaming by fetching these keyvalue sequentially, could also support jumping/seeking and previews.

Comments are very easy to do.


Discovery and "algorithm" is more difficult. 
However could take a more curated approach to this and have the site administrator highlight good videos + 
rely on some kind of curation subscription services where curators rate videos and you follow their recommendations if you like their taste.

Subscriptions to already known channels should also work.

Seems like it could be feasible
